Let's go shopping

In order not to spend excessively, we will buy only what we really need:
Let's start by determining how much we want to spend.
The second thing will be that we need to buy and what its real price is.
Importantly, the price must include cents, that is, hundredths.
If the shopping list exceeds how much we want to spend, we must correct
the list by crossing out the product that we can do without.
